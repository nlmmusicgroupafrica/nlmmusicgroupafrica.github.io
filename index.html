DoskiWaAfrika

An upcoming Afro and Deep House DJ/Producer from Koster, North-West, South Africa. Known for blending rich African rhythms with deep, emotive house grooves that move both hearts and feet.

Afro House | Deep Tech

I'M MusiQ

A genre-blending artist merging classical piano with high-energy EDM. His sound combines emotive melodies and powerful electronic beats, creating a unique fusion of elegance and intensity. Inspired by both classical and modern dance music, he crafts immersive tracks that captivate listeners and energize crowds.

Amapiano | EDM

T.fly deDeejay

Tomorrowland Anthems Album

Brace yourself for a seismic clash of sounds—where Amapiano’s hypnotic grooves collide with EDM’s explosive drops! Tomorrowland Anthems isn’t just a remix album; it’s a global dancefloor revolution. I'M MusiQ takes iconic festival bangers and injects them with Afro-house swagger, rewiring them with log-drum rhythms, shimmering synths, and basslines that hit like lightning.

The King Of Drum Tech Vol 02

DoskiWaAfrika cements his legacy with The King Of Drum Tech Vol 02, a full-length album that expands the boundaries of Afro House and Drum Tech. Building on the raw energy of Vol 01, this project delivers a deeper, more immersive journey—packed with hypnotic rhythms, tribal intensity, and the gritty underground sound that has become DoskiWaAfrika's signature.

The King Of Drum Tech Vol 01

DoskiWaAfrika steps into the spotlight with "The King Of Drum Tech Vol 01", a bootleg EP that showcases his raw and uncompromising take on Afro House and Drum Tech. Packed with hypnotic rhythms, deep tribal energy, and authentic underground flair, this collection reimagines sounds from the streets of Africa to the dance floors of the world.

This unofficial drop is a tribute to the pulse of Drum Tech culture, blending signature percussion with gritty edits and soulful echoes. Though not commercially released, this EP stands as a bold artistic statement from a rising voice in the scene.
