Music Releases

The King Of Drum Tech Vol 01

DoskiWaAfrika steps into the spotlight with "The King Of Drum Tech Vol 01", a bootleg EP that showcases his raw and uncompromising take on Afro House and Drum Tech. Packed with hypnotic rhythms, deep tribal energy, and authentic underground flair, this collection reimagines sounds from the streets of Africa to the dance floors of the world.

This unofficial drop is a tribute to the pulse of Drum Tech culture, blending signature percussion with gritty edits and soulful echoes. Though not commercially released, this EP stands as a bold artistic statement from a rising voice in the scene.
